Holiday Recipe – Fruit Cocktail

RICH & CREAMY FRUIT COCKTAIL

Ingredients:

2 Cups canned fruit cocktail

1 Tetrapack all purpose cream

(about 1 Cup)

½ bar cream cheese softened

Directions:

In a glass bowl, drain fruit cocktail. Discard the syrup. In a separate bowl, mix together butter, all-purpose cream and cream cheese. Blend till smooth. (Or you can leave some cream cheese bits for added texture) Pour into the fruit cocktail and gently toss till combined. Chill.
